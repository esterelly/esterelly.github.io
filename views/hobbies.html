Hobbies

Digital Art

I've loved creating art for as long as I can remember. I now sell my work online and at conventions around the US. I try to incorporate art in as many aspects of my life as I can. In fact, the logo and background for this website are drawn by me!

Nails

I do my own gel-x nails and enjoy making intricate and unique nail art in a variety of styles. It is a creative outlet that brings me joy.

Cello

Music has always been a passion of mine, and after dabbling in the piano and ukulele, I fell in love with the cello. I took cello lessons for five years and have also played in youth orchestras. I hope to join a college orchestra to hone my skills!
